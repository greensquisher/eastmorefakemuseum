Experience the Majesty of Ancient Egypt at Eastmore Historic Museum. ​

Get ready to embark on a thrilling journey through time and discover the fascinating world of ancient Egypt! Eastmore Historic Museum is proud to present its latest exhibit, "The Golden Sands of the Nile: Ancient Egyptian Wonders".

​

For a limited time only, explore the intricate tombs, majestic temples, and grand cities of this ancient civilization. Our exhibit features an impressive collection of artifacts and relics, including:

​

• The stunning golden mask of Pharaoh Tutankhamun, discovered in his tomb by Howard Carter in 1922

• An accurate replica of the Great Sphinx, guarding the entrance to the pyramid complex

• The tomb of a noble Egyptian, complete with colorful frescoes and hieroglyphics

• An impressive collection of mummies, including the famous mummy of Ramses II

• A vast array of funerary objects, including sarcophagi, canopic jars, and amulets

​

Our expert curators have carefully curated these artifacts to transport you back in time to a world of pharaohs, pyramids, and gods. Learn about the daily life, culture, and traditions of ancient Egyptians through interactive exhibits, films, and artifacts.

​

Don't miss this unique opportunity to delve into the history and mystery of one of the world's most fascinating civilizations. Visit Eastmore Historic Museum today and discover why ancient Egypt continues to captivate us all.
